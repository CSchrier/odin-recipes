Stove-top Pizza

Pizza is one of my favorite meals to cook but more importantly EAT.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese Selection such as fresh Mozzarella, or some italian blended preshred will do great
  • Toppings toppings toppings! I keep it simple and I recommend you do too, especially if they're high in mosture content
  • Cornmeal
  • Olive oil

Steps

  • Preheat your oven to 450 and your cast iron on the stove top on medium high
  • Have your toppings ready as once this processe starts you must keep going
  • Once everything is heated, dust the pan with cornmeal
  • Spread your dough from edge to edge of the pan
  • Spread 1-2 tbsp olive oil onto the top of the dough
  • Leave the dough to cook on the stove top until bubbles start for form on the top side of the dough
  • Quickly spread your desired amount of sauce, cheese, and toppings
  • Move the cast iron into the oven
  • Watch the cheese, once it reaches the desired doneness you must be ready to pull. I like some light browning to form.
